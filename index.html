Background

For our Iterative Design project, we choose Discz from Y combinator. The core conecpt behind Discz is that it is a Spotify-integrated music discovery platform built to help you find your next favorite artists and songs. It's main functionality is the ability to build playlists at the swipe of a finger and find new music via custom recommendations catered to your taste! You can swipe through song snippets to find music you love and add it to your spotify playlists.

Critiques

  • Create button, not sure what it's supposed to be doing since it links back to Discover. It should go to a Create page
  • Likes the hierarchy of it, especially with the album covers. The text is kind of small(describing the genres), but the big pictures are good.
  • Like the general layout, big album pictures. Intuition is to add the songs directly from the libraries, so when he sees the playlists he didn't find the discover button at the bottom, so he was looking for the plus sign in the libraries page.
  • Bottom bar is a different color, that's cool. It helps keep track of what page you're on. Discover and the playlists are a very similar format.
  • Likes the overall layout. Recommendations and recent listening are within the settings option of the app, it's a bit unintuitive.
  • Confused why the recommendations have a plus or minus on the settings screen, though the recommendations were all the songs you haven't listened to. Gordon - likes the navigation, it works well
  • Discover is similar to the library where you have different playlists. Didn't expect the discover to go into a song, thought it would go to a playlist
  • You get a heart, but couldn't find in the profile any connection to the heart. It's probably better to add a collection of playlists?
  • For the discovery page, the search should be on the outside. Add button => search isn't intuitive.
  • Confused about the mental model of when we're swiping on the tracks, does skipping it also mean liking or disliking? Because skipping goes to the right and left.
  • likes transition between pages. The plus icon on the Discover page is counterintuitive. It would be more clear if it was like “Explore more music” or something
  • Like the overall layout, confused about the minus and plus sign in the profile section and the little eye button in “my recent listens.” It would be better if the style of text input for usernames and names were more consistent.
  • The little detail you added, on the swiping screen you can see the next album behind it, which shows what you should do. You should swipe in some direction to see the next album.
  • like the hierarchy and transitional animations. Confused about the cancel button in the player screen (the X)
